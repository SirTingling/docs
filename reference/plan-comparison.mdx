## Overage

Developer and Pro plans charge **$0.02 per credit** over the included amount. You won't be cut off mid-request. Usage is billed at the end of the billing cycle.

Enterprise plans include unlimited credits. No overage charges.

Free plans are hard-capped. When you hit the limit, requests return `403 forbidden` until the next billing cycle.
