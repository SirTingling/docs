## Credit costs

Every operation consumes credits from your included balance.

| Operation | Credits |
|-----------|---------|
| Shield scan | 1 |
| Sentinel review | 2 |
| Fix suggestion | 3 |
| Code review | 5 |
| Policy evaluation | 1 |

## Overage

Developer and Pro plans charge **$0.02 per credit** over the included amount. You won't be cut off mid-request. Usage is billed at the end of the billing cycle.

Enterprise plans include unlimited credits. No overage charges.

Free plans are hard-capped. When you hit the limit, requests return `403 FORBIDDEN` with the code `PLAN_LIMIT_REACHED` until the next billing cycle.
