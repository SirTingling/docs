The Team page gives you a high-level view of your organization's members -- who is active, how many scans each person has run, seat utilization, and pending invitations.

<Note>This is not the same as **Settings > Members**, which is where you invite and manage members. The Team page is a read-oriented overview of your team's activity.</Note>

---

## Stats bar

Four summary metrics appear at the top:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total members** | Active members in the organization (excludes deleted members) |
| **Admins** | Number of active members with the Admin role |
| **Pending invites** | Outstanding invitations that haven't been accepted or expired |
| **Active today** | Members whose `lastActiveAt` timestamp falls within today |

The seat gauge shows how many of your plan's member seats are used (active members + pending invitations vs. the plan limit).

---

## Member table

Each row shows:

- **Name and email** with avatar
- **Role** (Owner, Admin, Security Lead, Billing Admin, Member, Viewer)
- **Status** (Active, Pending)
- **Last active** timestamp
- **Scan count** -- total scans attributed to this member, including scans made via API keys the member created

Scan counts are aggregated from `CreditUsage` records for `shield_scan`, `sentinel_review`, and `policy_eval` operations. API-key-attributed scans (no direct user ID) are resolved back to the key's creator.

---

## Filtering and sorting

- **Search** by name or email
- **Filter** by role or status
- **Sort** by name, role, last active date, or scan count

---

## Actions

Depending on your role, you can perform actions directly from the member table:

- **Change role** -- reassign a member's role (subject to anti-escalation rules)
- **Remove member** -- remove a member from the organization
- **Resend invitation** -- re-send the invite email for pending members
- **Revoke invitation** -- cancel a pending invitation

These actions use the same endpoints as **Settings > Members**.

---

## Permissions

The Team page is accessible to all authenticated members. Role changes and member removal require appropriate permissions (see [Team Management](/dashboard/members) for role details).
