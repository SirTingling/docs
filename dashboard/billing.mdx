Takumo uses a credit-based system. Each plan includes a credit allowance. Operations consume credits.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Included Credits |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | 1,000/mo |
| Developer | $29/mo | 5,000/mo |
| Pro | $39/mo per seat | 5,000/seat/mo |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited |

## Credit Costs per Operation

Every operation that Takumo performs consumes a specific number of credits:

| Operation | Credits |
|-----------|---------|
| Shield scan | 1 |
| Sentinel review | 2 |
| Fix suggestion | 3 |
| Code review | 5 |
| Policy evaluation | 1 |
| Real-time scan | 1 |

A Shield scan is the cheapest operation at 1 credit. A full code review is the most expensive at 5 credits. Most day-to-day usage is Shield scans and policy evaluations, both at 1 credit each.

## Overage

What happens when you exceed your monthly credit allowance depends on your plan:

| Plan | Overage Behavior |
|------|-----------------|
| Free | Hard limit. Operations are blocked until the next billing cycle. |
| Developer | $0.02 per credit over allowance |
| Pro | $0.02 per credit over allowance |
| Enterprise | Included. No overage charges. |

<Note>Pro plan credits scale with your team. 5 seats = 25,000 credits/month.</Note>

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Settings > Billing** in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a plan">
    Choose the plan you want to upgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The upgrade takes effect immediately. You get access to the new plan's features and credit allowance right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period. You keep access to your current plan's features until then.

If you're downgrading from a plan that includes features you're actively using (e.g., fleet management on Pro, SSO on Enterprise), those features will become unavailable when the downgrade takes effect.

## Monitoring Usage

The Billing page shows:

- **Current plan** and renewal date
- **Credits used** this billing period vs. your allowance
- **Credit burn rate** -- how quickly you're consuming credits based on recent usage
- **Overage charges** -- any charges beyond your included allowance
